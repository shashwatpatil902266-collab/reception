## 🗓️ Major Gold Price Cycles (1971-Present)

### Cycle 1: The 1970s Bull Market (1971-1980)
- **Start:** $35/oz (end of Bretton Woods)
- **Peak:** $850/oz (Jan 1980)
- **Return:** +2,300%
- **Drivers:** End of gold standard, oil crisis, Iran hostage crisis, hyperinflation
- **End:** Volcker raised US rates to 20%

### Cycle 2: The Lost Decade (1980-2001)
- **Period:** 21 years of bear / sideways
- **Low:** $252/oz (Aug 1999)
- **Drivers:** Strong dollar, disinflation, tech boom pulled capital away from gold

### Cycle 3: The Secular Bull (2001-2011)
- **Start:** $252/oz
- **Peak:** $1,920/oz (Sep 2011)
- **Return:** +660%
- **Drivers:** Dot-com crash, 9/11, Fed easing, 2008 financial crisis, QE

### Cycle 4: Consolidation (2011-2018)
- **Period:** 7 years of sideways/down
- **Low:** $1,050/oz (Dec 2015)
- **Drivers:** Fed taper talk, strong dollar, China slowdown

### Cycle 5: Current Bull Market (2019-Present)
- **Start:** $1,300/oz
- **Current peak:** $5,595/oz (Jan 2026)
- **Return so far:** +330%
- **Drivers:** Covid, central bank buying, de-dollarization, geopolitics, debasement trade

## 📈 Historical Price Reactions — Pattern Library

### Pattern 1: Fed Rate Cut Cycles
**Historical examples:**
- **2001-2003:** Fed cut rates from 6.5% to 1% → gold rose from $270 to $420 (+56%)
- **2007-2008:** Fed cut from 5.25% to 0.25% → gold rose from $650 to $1,000 (+54%)
- **2019-2020:** Fed cut cycle → gold rose from $1,300 to $2,070 (+59%)

**Current parallel:** If Fed cuts 150bps in 2026, historical template suggests ~40-50% gold rally

### Pattern 2: Geopolitical Crises
**Historical events & gold reaction:**
| Event | Year | Gold Move | Duration |
|-------|------|-----------|----------|
| Iran Hostage Crisis | 1979-80 | +120% | 1 year |
| Gulf War I | 1990 | +8% | 2 months |
| 9/11 Attacks | 2001 | +6% | 1 week |
| Gulf War II | 2003 | +11% | 1 month |
| Russia-Ukraine | 2022 | +14% | 1 month |
| Israel-Hamas | 2023 | +7% | 2 weeks |
| Iran War | 2026 | +18% | 2 weeks |

**Rule:** Initial spike fades 50-70% within 3 months unless conflict persists

### Pattern 3: Inflation Surprises
**Historical CPI shock → gold reaction:**
- **CPI beats by 0.3%+:** Gold rallies 1-2% same day
- **CPI misses by 0.3%+:** Gold drops 1-2% same day
- **Sustained high inflation (6+ months):** Gold gains 20-40% over 12 months

### Pattern 4: Central Bank Buying Cycles
**Historical central bank buying waves:**
- **2010-2012:** Russia, India build reserves → gold rose from $1,100 to $1,900
- **2018-2019:** Russia, China, Turkey → gold rose from $1,200 to $1,550
- **2022-2026:** De-dollarization wave → gold rose from $1,800 to $5,500+

**Rule:** Sustained CB buying (>800 tonnes/year) historically = 15-25% annual gains

### Pattern 5: Dollar Weakness
**DXY major declines & gold:**
- **2002-2008:** DXY fell 40% → gold rose 300%
- **2017-2018:** DXY fell 10% → gold rose 15%
- **2020:** DXY fell 12% → gold rose 25%

**Rule of thumb:** Every 1% DXY decline = ~1.5-2% gold rise
