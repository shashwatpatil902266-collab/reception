# FACTORS REFERENCE — The 18 Drivers of Gold Price

This is the master knowledge base. Every prediction must consider these factors.
For each factor: check current state, assess direction, assign weight.

---

## 🏦 CATEGORY 1: MACRO-ECONOMIC (7 factors)

### F1. Inflation & Inflation Expectations
- **Direction rule:** Rising inflation = gold UP
- **How to check:** Search "India CPI latest" and "US CPI latest"
- **Why it matters:** Gold is the classic inflation hedge. When money loses value, gold gains.
- **Weight:** VERY HIGH

### F2. Real Interest Rates (#1 MOST IMPORTANT per PIMCO)
- **Formula:** Real Rate = Nominal Interest Rate − Inflation Rate
- **Direction rule:** Falling real rates = gold UP
- **How to check:** US 10-year Treasury yield minus US inflation expectations
- **Why it matters:** Gold pays no interest. When bonds pay less (real terms), gold becomes relatively more attractive.
- **Weight:** VERY HIGH

### F3. US Dollar Strength (DXY)
- **Direction rule:** Strong USD = gold DOWN; Weak USD = gold UP
- **How to check:** Search "DXY current" or "dollar index today"
- **Why it matters:** Gold is priced in USD globally. Weak dollar = gold cheaper for non-US buyers = more demand.
- **India-specific:** Also check USD/INR. A weak rupee makes imported gold MORE expensive in India even if global price falls.
- **Weight:** HIGH

### F4. Federal Reserve / RBI Monetary Policy
- **Direction rule:** Rate cuts / QE = gold UP; Rate hikes / QT = gold DOWN
- **How to check:** Latest FOMC statement, RBI MPC decisions
- **Why it matters:** Lower rates reduce opportunity cost of holding gold.
- **Weight:** VERY HIGH

### F5. Opportunity Cost (Bond Yields vs Gold)
- **Direction rule:** High yields = investors prefer bonds = gold DOWN
- **How to check:** US 10-year yield, Indian G-Sec 10-year yield
- **Weight:** HIGH

### F6. Global Money Supply (M2)
- **Direction rule:** Expanding money supply = currency debasement = gold UP
- **How to check:** Global M2 data, central bank balance sheet size
- **Weight:** MEDIUM-HIGH (structural, slow-moving)

### F7. Economic Growth / Recession Fears
- **Direction rule:** Recession fears = gold UP; Strong growth = gold DOWN
- **How to check:** GDP data, jobs reports, PMI indices
- **Weight:** HIGH

## 🌍 CATEGORY 2: GEOPOLITICAL & NEWS (3 factors)

### F8. Wars, Conflicts & Geopolitical Crises
- **Direction rule:** Any major conflict = gold UP (safe-haven demand)
- **How to check:** Latest headlines on Iran, Ukraine, Middle East, Taiwan
- **Weight:** HIGH (short-term spikes)

### F9. Trade Wars, Tariffs & Sanctions
- **Direction rule:** Trade tensions = gold UP
- **How to check:** Trump tariff news, India-US trade talks, China trade disputes
- **Weight:** HIGH

### F10. Political Instability & Government Trust
- **Direction rule:** Political chaos = gold UP
- **How to check:** Election news, government crisis headlines
- **Weight:** MEDIUM

---

## 📊 CATEGORY 3: MARKET & INVESTOR BEHAVIOUR (5 factors)

### F11. Central Bank Gold Purchases
- **Direction rule:** Central banks buying = gold UP (structural)
- **How to check:** World Gold Council monthly data, RBI gold reserve updates
- **Weight:** VERY HIGH (structural support)

### F12. Gold ETF Flows
- **Direction rule:** ETF inflows = gold UP; outflows = gold DOWN
- **How to check:** SPDR Gold (GLD) holdings, Indian gold ETF AUM
- **Weight:** HIGH (fast-moving signal)

### F13. Market Momentum & Technical Sentiment
- **Direction rule:** Breaking resistance = continues UP; Breaking support = continues DOWN
- **How to check:** Moving averages (50-day, 200-day), RSI, Google Trends "gold price"
- **Weight:** MEDIUM-HIGH (short-term)

### F14. Stock Market Volatility (VIX)
- **Direction rule:** Rising VIX = gold UP (flight to safety)
- **How to check:** India VIX, US VIX
- **Weight:** MEDIUM

### F15. Bitcoin / Crypto Competition
- **Direction rule:** Bitcoin rallying as "digital gold" = gold DOWN (minor); Bitcoin crashing = gold UP
- **How to check:** BTC/USD price, crypto ETF flows
- **Weight:** LOW-MEDIUM

---

## ⛏️ CATEGORY 4: SUPPLY-SIDE (2 factors)

### F16. Gold Mining Production & Costs
- **Direction rule:** Higher mining costs / lower production = gold UP
- **How to check:** Quarterly reports from major miners (Newmont, Barrick, etc.)
- **Weight:** LOW-MEDIUM (slow-moving)

### F17. Gold Recycling & Secondary Supply
- **Direction rule:** High recycling supply = gold DOWN; low recycling = gold UP
- **India-specific:** Indians pledging gold for loans reduces recycling supply
- **Weight:** LOW-MEDIUM

---

## 💍 CATEGORY 5: DEMAND-SIDE (1 factor - covers jewelry, industrial, seasonal)

### F18. Jewelry, Industrial & Seasonal Demand
- **Direction rule:** Festival/wedding season = gold UP
- **India-specific calendar:**
  - **Oct-Dec (wedding season):** HIGH demand
  - **Akshaya Tritiya (April-May):** HIGH demand spike
  - **Dhanteras / Diwali (Oct-Nov):** HIGH demand spike
  - **Monsoon (Jun-Sep):** Rural demand tied to harvest outlook
  - **Jan-Feb:** Moderate demand
- **Import duty impact:** India's ~15% import duty means local prices stay elevated even when global prices fall
- **Weight:** MEDIUM (India-specific: HIGH)

## Quick-Reference Scoring Matrix

When analyzing, assign each factor:
- **+2** = Strongly bullish for gold
- **+1** = Mildly bullish
- **0** = Neutral
- **−1** = Mildly bearish
- **−2** = Strongly bearish

### Weighted Score Formula
```
Total Score = Σ (Factor_Score × Factor_Weight)

Weights:
- VERY HIGH = 3x
- HIGH = 2x
- MEDIUM = 1.5x
- LOW-MEDIUM = 1x
- LOW = 0.5x
```

### Score Interpretation
- **+30 to +45:** Strong BUY signal (probability of rise: 75-85%)
- **+15 to +29:** Moderate BUY (probability: 60-75%)
- **+5 to +14:** Mild bullish (probability: 55-60%)
- **−4 to +4:** Neutral / HOLD (probability: 45-55%)
- **−5 to −14:** Mild bearish (probability: 40-45%)
- **−15 to −29:** Moderate SELL (probability: 25-40%)
- **−30 to −45:** Strong SELL (probability: 15-25%)
